About me

My name is Henry, I am a Computer Science student in Software Engineering track graduated from Purdue University in May 2019.

I have been coding for around 4 years now and I started to get involved in Web development about 2 years ago.

I was an undergraduate research assistant in Purdue University, being assigned to the app development team focusing on the implementation of Crack Inspection on buildings using Augmented Reality devices such as Microsoft Hololens.

Computer Science is a rapidly evolving field and I am excited for any new technology that is going to show up in the future. I am currently seeking for more opportunities in the professional fields to widen and sharpen my skills.
